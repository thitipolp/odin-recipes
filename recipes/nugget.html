McNugget Recipe

Here's the secret to making the famous McNugget. Now that you know, I'll have to kill you...

  1. Add your chicken, salt and one egg in a food processor. Blend until the mixture looks like a paste.
  2. APlace the corn starch in a bowl. With wet hands, shape a handful of the mixture into a nugget. Dip the nuggets into the corn starch and place it on a lined baking sheet.
  3. AIn a large bowl, combine the water, one egg, mustard, spices and flour. Place the nuggets in the batter and place back onto the baking sheet.
  4. APlace the sheet in the freezer and allow them to freeze for 1 hour.
  5. AHeat your oil to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Deep fry the first batch for 5 minutes. Allow the nuggets to drain on paper towel and deep fry again for 5 minutes. This helps the chicken cook through without burning it.
  6. AServe the nuggets with a sauce of your choice.
